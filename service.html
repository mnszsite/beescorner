Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • What are the visa requirements for PNG citizens traveling to the Philippines? Papua New Guinea citizens can travel to the Philippines without a visa for stays of up to 30 days. Please ensure your passport is valid for at least six months upon entry.
  • Is it safe for PNG travelers to visit the Philippines? Yes, the Philippines is a popular destination for travelers worldwide. Our guides prioritize your safety by ensuring you are well-informed and supported throughout your journey.

Safety & Travel Tips

  • Health and Safety: Make sure to check any health advisories and vaccinations before traveling. We recommend carrying personal medical supplies and always drinking bottled water.
  • Local Customs: Be respectful of local traditions and customs. Always ask before taking photos, especially in sacred or private areas.
  • Emergency Numbers: In case of emergency, dial 911 in the Philippines for immediate assistance.
  • Climate: The Philippines has a tropical climate, so pack light clothing and sunscreen. Stay hydrated and be aware of seasonal weather patterns, especially during the monsoon season.
